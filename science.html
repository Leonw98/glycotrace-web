GlycoTrace White Paper

Architectural Proof for Metabolic Stability & Forensic Data Auditing

1. Executive Statement

GlycoTrace is a retrospective metabolic analysis platform designed to bridge the gap between "Raw Data" and "Clinical Insight." Developed under the "Product Founder" mindset, it addresses the specific fragmentation challenges faced by Type 3c Diabetics and individuals with ADHD, where data discipline is the primary engine for health.

2. Technical Methodology: "Security-as-Code"

We utilise a Zero-Client-Token Architecture to protect sensitive health data (PII/PHI).

  • LibreLinkUp Proxy: The user's LibreLink bearer token never touches the mobile device. Authentication and synchronisation are handled server-side via Supabase Edge Functions, reducing the attack surface to zero on the client side.
  • Hybrid Reservoir Search: Local "Staple" matching (0ms) followed by a debounced (400ms) query to our MotherDuck Global Reservoir.

3. The "Brain": Metabolic Logic & Formulas

A. Momentum Analysis ($G_v$)

The core differentiator of GlycoTrace is Glucose Velocity ($G_v$).

Formula: $G_v = (G_{now} - G_{30m}) / 0.5$ (expressed in mmol/L per hour).

The AI distinguishes between exogenous spikes (carb-heavy meals) and endogenous "stress dumps" (anaerobic exercise) by correlating $G_v$ with recent log data.

B. The Phantom Auditor 3.0

Standard CGM algorithms often suffer from "sensor lag" or false low alarms. The Auditor performs a retrospective comparison between live "alert" values and stable historical CSV data to identify "near misses" and algorithm flaws.

C. Insulin Safety: 4-Hour Linear Decay

GlycoTrace rejects the "flat sum" model of insulin tracking. We use a 4-hour clinical linear decay model for Active Insulin (IOB) to prevent dangerous "insulin stacking."

Glycaemic Variability (CV%)

Formula: CV = (SD / Mean) x 100

Stable threshold: < 36% (Danne et al., 2017). CV% is the internationally recommended metric because it is independent of the mean glucose level.

The GLUT4 Translocation Logic

Muscle contraction translocates GLUT4 transporters to the cell surface independently of insulin signalling (Richter & Hargreaves, 2013). This additive effect creates compound hypo risk during exercise, which GlycoTrace flags via its Post-Exercise Sensitivity Boost analysis.

GMI (Estimated HbA1c)

The Glucose Management Indicator provides an estimate of your lab HbA1c based on CGM data, using the validated formula established by Bergenstal et al. (2018).

Philosophy

Data Ethics & Research Philosophy

No Sale of Personal Data

GlycoTrace was born as a personal data project. We maintain a fundamental policy: We do not sell individual user data to third parties, advertisers, or data brokers.

Open Science Commitment

We believe in the power of Open Science. We partner with accredited research bodies to advance the understanding of glucose velocity ($G_v$) and insulin-to-carb efficiency in real-world settings.

Anonymisation Shields

  • Level 1 (Aggregated): High-level trend analysis (for example, "average spike for high-protein meals") with zero identification risk.
  • Level 2 (De-identified): Research-only datasets where PII is stripped, timestamps are jittered, and UUIDs are masked.
