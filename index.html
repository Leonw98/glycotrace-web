Metabolic Questions Answered

Why is Type 3c management different?

Type 3c involves both insulin and glucagon deficiency, making stability much harder to predict. GlycoTrace highlights the subtle patterns other apps miss.

Are there differences for men and women?

Yes. Women often experience significant fluctuations in insulin sensitivity during different phases of their hormonal cycle. We build this context into our analysis.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. GlycoTrace is a retrospective analysis tool. We sync your data securely to provide insights, but you remain the sole owner of your health journey.
