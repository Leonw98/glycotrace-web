The Backstory

I built this because I was tired of jumping between apps to find the 'Why'.

GlycoTrace started as a personal project. When I was diagnosed with Type 3c diabetes, I quickly realized how much of our care is a "missing puzzle." I discovered vital treatments like Enzyme Therapy through my own researchï¿½it was never mentioned by my clinicians, and I know others in the UK facing the same silence.

I built GlycoTrace because I was tired of jumping between four different apps just to find the patterns that mattered.: the impact of every meal, the timing of every dose, and the often-ignored enzyme treatments. But it didn't stop there.

Whether you're T1, T2, T3c, or simply a wellness enthusiast looking for a clean, fast calorie tracker to see how food impacts your blood sugar, GlycoTrace is designed for you. I want to make metabolic health logical, accessible, and community-driven.
