The Backstory

I built this because I was tired of jumping between apps to find the 'Why'.

GlycoTrace started as a personal project. When I was diagnosed with Type 3c diabetes, I quickly realised how much of our care is a "missing puzzle." I discovered vital treatments like Enzyme Therapy through my own research: it was never mentioned by my clinicians, and I know others in the UK facing the same silence.

I built GlycoTrace to move from reactive monitoring to Retrospective Auditing. By comparing live sensor data against backfilled CSV truth-data, we identify the patterns and "Near Misses" that standard alarms can often miss.

Whether you're T1, T2, T3c, or simply looking for a clear way to track how your lifestyle impacts your metabolism, GlycoTrace is designed for you. I want to make metabolic health logical, accessible, and community-driven.

Metabolic Momentum

The Math of Flow: Identifying change before the sensor screams.

A simple logbook can't handle metabolic momentum. We treat your biology like an enterprise data problem, tracking the Rate of Change to distinguish between a dietary spike and exercise-induced stress.

By analysing the velocity of your glucose trends, Glycoflow identifies the patterns that standard alarms often miss, giving you the clarity to act with precision.

How it helps

Features for Everyone.

⚡

The Phantom Auditor

Identifying "Near Misses" by reconciling live LLU streams against backfilled CSV truth-data for absolute precision.

💊

Enzyme Tracker

Unique support for Type 3c and pancreatic insufficiency. Track your enzyme doses alongside your meals for better absorption mapping.

📸

Zero-Latency Garmin Import

I deliberately dropped the slow 6-hour Health Connect sync. Instead, import stats instantly via **on-device OCR** from your Garmin or fitness watch screen. No waiting, just precision.

🔥

Fasting Intelligence

Safety-Aware timers with an automatic "Safety Pivot" that prioritises metabolic stability if glucose drops below 4.0 mmol/L.

🥗

ADHD-Optimized Logging

Designed for speed and "Bulk Logging." A fast, clean interface for logging food and exercise, making it easy to catch up if you've missed a few entries.

🧠

Glycoflow AI 2.0

Incorporating Momentum Analysis to distinguish between dietary spikes and exercise stress. High-fidelity auditing with separate Basal/Bolus averages for clear sensitivity insights.
