What is Type 3c Diabetes?

Type 3c diabetes, also known as pancreatogenic diabetes, is often misdiagnosed as Type 2. However, its origin is physical damage to the pancreas. This means the body isn't just "resistant" to insulin; it has lost the complex machinery that produces it, alongside the glucagon and digestive enzymes needed for stability.

For a lifter or athlete, this creates a "Double Jeopardy" scenario. You need the enzymes to absorb the protein for muscle growth, but those same enzymes change how quickly carbohydrates hit your system. GlycoTrace is the first app to put enzyme tracking at the center of the timeline.

Managing Heavy Lifting & Blood Sugar

When you lift heavy, your body enters a "fight or flight" mode. This triggers the liver to dump glucose into the bloodstream to fuel your muscles. For those with Type 3c, this spike can be sharp and intimidating. The key is understanding Glucoflow AI's distinction between "dietary fuel" and "stress fuel."

By tracking your lifting sets alongside your glucose trends, GlycoTrace helps you identify your specific "spike threshold," allowing you to stay in the zone without the fear of uncontrolled highs or the "post-gym crash."
