# GlycoTrace: Metabolic Analysis Platform

GlycoTrace is a specialized retrospective metabolic analysis platform designed for FreeStyle Libre users, with specific expertise in Type 3c Diabetes, enzyme therapy, and high-performance weightlifting.

## Core Philosophy
- **Retrospective Auditing:** Moving from reactive, real-time monitoring to post-hoc analysis. Identifying "Near Misses" by reconciling live sensor data (LibreLinkUp) with backfilled CSV "ground truth."
- **Momentum Analysis ($G_{v}$):** Factoring in Glucose Velocity to understand trends (upward/downward) before making dosing or dietary interventions.
- **Elite Data Discipline:** A minimalist, ADHD-friendly approach to chronic health data that reduces the mental burden of management.

## Key Features
- **The Phantom Auditor:** A synchronization engine that identifies discrepancies between asynchronous sensor streams and manual logs.
- **Safety-Aware Fasting:** Automatic "Safety Pivot" logic that pauses fasting timers if blood glucose drops below 4.0 mmol/L to prioritize stability.
- **Separation of Averages:** Critical UI distinction between Basal and Bolus averages to provide clear insulin sensitivity insights.
- **Enzyme Therapy Tracking:** Specialized tracking for pancreatic insufficiency (Type 3c).
- **OCR Garmin Import:** Automated exercise logging from Garmin screenshots via on-device text recognition.

## Target Audience
- **Type 3c Diabetics:** Those managing pancreatic-related metabolic conditions.
- **High-Performance Athletes:** Weightlifters and professionals using data to optimize metabolic stability during physical stress.
- **Wellness Enthusiasts:** Individuals seeking a clean, logical calorie and glucose tracker without medical jargon.

## Technical Standards
- High-contrast mmol/L graphs.
- Color-coded glucose thresholds:
  - Red: <3.9 mmol/L (Hypo risk)
  - Green: 3.9 - 10.0 mmol/L (Stability range)
  - Amber: 10.1 - 13.0 mmol/L (Mild spike)
  - Dark Red: >13.0 mmol/L (High risk)
