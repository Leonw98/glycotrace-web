Safety First

Community Guidelines

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

1. No Medical Advice

GlycoTrace is a community for sharing retrospective data and personal experiences. **Users are strictly prohibited from providing or soliciting medical advice.** Any information shared by peers is for educational and informational purposes only.

Always consult your doctor, diabetes specialist, or healthcare provider before making any changes to your:

  • Insulin dosages or timing
  • Enzyme therapy (PERT) regimes
  • Dietary plans
  • Strenuous exercise routines

2. Liability Waiver

By using GlycoTrace and participating in its community features, you agree that you do so at your own risk. GlycoTrace Ltd and its directors are not liable for any health complications or outcomes resulting from following peer-shared "tips" or insights.

3. Respect and Privacy

We are a community built on trust. Do not share the personal data of other users. Be respectful, especially when discussing the challenges of Type 3c, chronic illness, and recovery.

4. Reporting Dangerous Content

If you see a post or comment that encourages dangerous medical behavior, use the "Report" feature immediately. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for misinformation that puts lives at risk.
