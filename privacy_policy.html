Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 24, 2026

Your Data is Yours.

At GlycoTrace, we believe your health data is private. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Information We Collect

We collect metabolic data (glucose, insulin, meals) only for the purpose of providing retrospective analysis and patterns to you.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is synced securely with our backend for processing by our analysis engine and Glucoflow AI. We do not use your data for advertising.

Cookies and Tracking

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. This involves the use of cookies to collect anonymous information such as page views and session duration. You can opt-out of tracking via your browser settings.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app settings.
